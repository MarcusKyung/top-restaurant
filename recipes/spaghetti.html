Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a beloved Italian pasta dish featuring long, thin cylindrical noodles made from durum wheat semolina. This classic dish is known for its simplicity and versatility, often served with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, Alfredo, or pesto. Spaghetti is cooked until al dente, providing a perfect texture that complements the chosen sauce. It's a staple in Italian cuisine and enjoyed worldwide for its delicious taste and endless customization options.
